The Virtue Expedition

Widget Gallery · for Canvas embedding

This page is for teachers and admins. It previews every widget in the expedition's design system so you can verify what will appear in Canvas before you embed it.

Three widgets are available: the Journey Map (top-of-page landscape), the Virtue Header (activity title card), and the Passage Stamp (reward moment). Copy the URL from any preview below to embed that state in Canvas. Full embed instructions are in the README.

Journey Map

The illustrated landscape banner at the top of every activity page. Shows the scholar's progress through all seven virtues as landmarks on a trail. Parameters: ?current=, ?completed=, ?grade=.

Beginning the expedition

On Courage, nothing completed yet. ?current=courage

Halfway through

Three earned, on Courtesy. ?current=courtesy&completed=courage,honesty,responsibility

Expedition complete

All seven earned. ?completed=courage,honesty,responsibility,courtesy,service,perseverance,selfgovt

Virtue Header

The archway title card for the top of each activity, below the Journey Map. Shows the station pill, activity name, big headline, and a tagline. Each virtue has its own faint background illustration. Parameters: ?virtue=, ?station=, optional ?headline= and ?tagline=.

Courage · Station 1 (custom headline)

?virtue=courage&station=1&headline=What+is+courage%2C+really%3F

Courage · Station 2 (custom headline + tagline)

Custom text about the story. ?virtue=courage&station=2&headline=The+Lamp-Lighter's+Daughter&tagline=...

Honesty · Station 1

Different virtue, different illustration. ?virtue=honesty&station=1

Perseverance · Station 5 (defaults)

No custom headline — the widget fills in defaults. ?virtue=perseverance&station=5

Passage Stamp

The celebratory reward moment at the end of a completed station, before “Up Next.” Animated: seal enters with a small bounce, then a glow ring pulses quietly. Parameters: ?virtue=, ?station=.
